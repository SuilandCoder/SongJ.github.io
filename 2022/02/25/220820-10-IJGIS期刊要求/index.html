1 论文结构

Your paper should be compiled in the following order:

  • title page;
  • abstract;
  • keywords;
  • main text introduction, materials and methods, results, discussion;
  • declaration of interest statement;
  • references;
  • appendices (as appropriate).

2 字数限制

需要写明论文字数。

字数无限制，平均18-20页，5000-8000字；

6 Check List

  • Author details.

  • unstructured abstract of 200 words.

  • Between 2 and 5 keywords.

  • Funding details.

    • For single agency grants
      This work was supported by the [Funding Agency] under Grant [number xxxx].
    • For multiple agency grants
      This work was supported by the [Funding Agency #1] under Grant [number xxxx]; [Funding Agency #2] under Grant [number xxxx]; and [Funding Agency #3] under Grant [number xxxx].

  • Data and Codes Availability Statement.

    • 数据和代码匿名处理，上传云存储Figshare or Dryad；
    • 结论后面加 Data and Codes Availability Statement
      • The data and codes that support the findings of this study are available with the identifier(s) at the private link (give the PRIVATE SHARING LINK, such as https://figshare.com/articles/dataset/DataAndCodes_zip/19236129).
    • 一旦你的稿件被暂时接受，你应该提供一个带有DOI的永久链接，并更新数据和代码可用性声明。你也可以在数据和代码中添加作者的名字。

  • Figures

    • 为便于同行评审，图和表应插入主文件中。接受后，作者应按以下方式准备制作数字。图形应是高质量的（线条艺术为1200dpi，灰度为600dpi，彩色为300dpi，尺寸正确）。图案应以我们喜欢的文件格式之一提供。EPS、PS、JPEG、TIFF或Microsoft Word（DOC或DOCX）文件均可用于用Word绘制的图片。有关其他文件类型的信息，请查阅我们的电子作品提交文件。
